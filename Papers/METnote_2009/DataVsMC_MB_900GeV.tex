\section{Data to Monte Carlo comparisons at $\sqrt{s}=900$ GeV in
  Minimum Bias events}
\label{sc:DataVsMCMB900}

In this section we present the comparison of several $\etmiss$
distributions in data with Monte Carlo simulation. All distributions
shown in this section are required to have pass the events selections
described in Sec.4.
